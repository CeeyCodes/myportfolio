01 — About

Design with intention,
code with precision

Hi, I'm Cynthia — a multidisciplinary creative based in Nigeria. I work at the intersection of development and design, building web experiences that are both visually compelling and functionally robust.

With a background spanning frontend engineering, UI/UX strategy, and visual communication, I bring a holistic lens to every project. I believe great design is invisible — it just works, and it feels right.

When I'm not shipping code or wireframing, you'll find me exploring generative art, typography, and the philosophy of human-centered design.
